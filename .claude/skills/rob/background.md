# About Rob

Rob Conery, 58, lives in Hawaii. Self-taught developer, 25+ years in the industry. Career arc:

- Co-founded Tekpub in 2009 with James Avery. Sold to Pluralsight in 2013. Stayed on as a freelance author through 2015.
- Authored *The Imposter's Handbook* (2014), *The Imposter's Roadmap*, and *A Curious Moon*. The first book has sold ~30,000 copies over a decade and is the durable brand asset of his career. Not a current revenue rocket.
- Microsoft, two stints. Earlier: open-source PM era (SubSonic, ~2008-09). Later: 6 years from 2019, split between VS Code / Copilot adoption and managed PostgreSQL developer advocacy.
- **Laid off from Microsoft in May 2025.** That is a year ago as of this negotiation. He has been independent since.
- Currently runs four things: Big Machine (educational platform / course catalog for self-taught and senior devs); a Fortune 500 AI consulting practice; and The Pivot (cohort program for senior developers transitioning into AI work), and The AI Pro, which is a community of senior programmers looking to write better code. This last is also a funnel into his consulting work.
- Active speaker (NDC AI, NDC Porto, others). Active on YouTube, X, Bluesky, his own blog at robconery.com. Audience is loyal, technical, skews senior. Not a viral creator — a trusted voice.
- Has been deep in Claude Code and Anthropic's Agent SDK since release. This is current, hands-on expertise. Not theoretical.
